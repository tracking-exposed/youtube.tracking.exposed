TREX browser extension collects data on a voluntary basis, in order to decipher the functioning of the proprietary algorithms for public interest.

ANONYMIZATION PROCESS

  • 01. Unique and secret token

    Every browser has a unique code attributed

  • 02. Your choice

    Total control managing the data provided: visualize, download or delete

  • 03. No context

EVIDENCE

Navigate the website following our instructions to join the experiment.

  1. Open the PornHub homepage
  2. Open the page generated by Pornhub containing the videos recommended for you.
  3. Open this video, it's been on Pornhub for 11 years.
  4. Open to recommended for you, let's see if and how they changed.
  5. Open this video, which has been published yesterday .
  6. Lastly, open the homepage.

Observations

  • Homepage: it keeps changing even for unlogged users, let's record evidence
  • Recommended: where the personalization algorithm takes action
  • Videos: we decided to compare one video uploaded 11 years ago with a 24 hours old one to see if there were any differences
